## Free

**0€/month** — No credit card required.

- 1 server
- 24 hours of metric history
- 30-second polling interval
- Server status, power, production, players, trains, drones panels
- No alerts, no API access, no historical charts beyond 24h

Best for: trying FICSIT.monitor or monitoring a single personal server where
24-hour history is sufficient.

---

## Hobby

**6€/month** (58€/year — ~20% off)

Everything in Free, plus:
- 2 servers
- 7 days of metric history
- 15-second polling interval (double the frequency of Free)
- Discord alert notifications

Best for: small groups of friends wanting recent history and basic Discord notifications.

---

## Pro

**19€/month** (182€/year — ~20% off)

Everything in Hobby, plus:
- 5 servers
- 30 days of metric history
- 10-second polling interval
- Multi-channel alerts (Discord + additional channels)
- **Public REST API** access (10,000 requests/day)
- **Shareable dashboard links** — share a read-only dashboard view with others
- Twitch/YouTube overlay widget
- Email support (response within 24 hours)

Best for: active server admins, content creators, and small community servers who
want historical charts, API integrations, and sharing capabilities.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**What happens to my data if I downgrade?**
Data collected while on a higher tier is retained until it expires under your new
tier's retention period. No data is deleted immediately on downgrade.

**Can I add more servers beyond my plan limit?**
No — you must upgrade to a higher tier to add more servers.

**Is there a free trial for paid plans?**
Contact us for trial access to Pro or Team plans.

**What counts as a "server"?**
Each unique Satisfactory dedicated server IP + port combination counts as one server.
Multiple instances on different ports of the same machine count as separate servers.
